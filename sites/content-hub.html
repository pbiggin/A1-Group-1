Know the Facts!

There are 4 main competitions which make up the top tier of Australian domestic cricket which include:

The Sheffield Shield

The Sheffield Shield is the domestic first-class cricket competition of Australia. This is palyed by the teams representing the six states of Australia. the title will go to the state who secures the most bonus points after the first innings.

one-day cup

The one-day cup aslso comonly known as the Marsh One-day Cup, It is an Australian domestic List A 50-over limited-overs cricket tournament. It is also played by the 6 teams of the different states.

Big Bash League

The Big Bash League has a Men’s and Woman tournament established in 2011 by cricket Australia. There are 8 Men’s and Woman teams that play for the title each season. (note the Male and Females do not play each other).

WNCL

The Womans National Cricket League is a domestsic 50-over tournaments for womans cricket. played by teams from the different states.
